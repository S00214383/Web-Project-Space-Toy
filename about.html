Among the different stages of development of children is the stage of imagining. This, which occurs from 36 months and lasts until 12 years of age, is the moment when children begin to understand what they like and don't like to play, make their first friends and create different games. In addition to starting to put into practice the things they learn at home, in schools and in all the social communities they attend. This is the stage of wasting energy, trying out different activities and finding what your little one most identifies with. At Toymania you will find all the toys you need to help develop children's imaginations, find musical instruments, board games, slime and more.
